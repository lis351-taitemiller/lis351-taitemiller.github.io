She is a duck who can transform into a beautiful princess who helps restore her beloved princes heart. I picked this anime for the nastolgia. This is one of my fist shows which made me fall in love. Its not well known, but netizens online have gave this anime a 10/10 stating "Overall, Princess Tutu is a living, breathing anime that, unlike most magical-shoujo shows, truly feels magical"-VivisQueen on . overall I rate this anime a 10/10!

    Kamisama Kiss (2008-2016) was made by the Viz Media. in total there was 25 volumes with over 40 episodes. The show follows Nanami Momozomo, an average girl who is coping with her fathers gambling adiction and is titering on the verge of homelessness. as a last hope she goes to pray and on the way helps save a dog from a tree. Her good deeds are rewared and Tomoe, a fox god, is sent sure to take care of her. Nanami and Tomoe find themselves in a quirky love with friends that join in along the way. This anime meets all of my qualifications and more. Tomoe is compassionate and gentle while Nanimi is loud and bubble. the pair make for great TV and have caused the show to go viral on many ocasions. I rate this show a 10/10.   3. Fruits Basket (

    Fruits Basket is a comedy/ romance anime centered around a young unhoused woman named Tohru Honda. Her life gets turned around after she meets the owners of the land which she is living on. The owners family is effected by a hereditary curse which turns wvery member into an animal of the Eastern Zodiac. This anime shows the heartwarming ways which Tohru finds her place within this dysfuctional family. Romance blooms within the house and we get to see the beauty in all diffrent types of people. This anime is amazing for beginers because its engaging and lighthearted. There is adult themes but they are portrayed in a palateble way. Tohru is a strong, smart woman, who stands up for the people she loves and calls it like it is. This anime is a cult classic, it has a Rotten Tomato rating of 97%! I rate this anime a 8.9/10 just because I have others on this list I prefer slightly.

    Rosario + Vampire (2008) is a much more adult and comedic romance. Sukune Aono is an average teenager who is unable to get into any high school because of his bad grades. His parents inadvertently enroll him into Youkai Academy as a last-ditch effort to secure his education. Oukai Academy is a seemingly normal boarding school, except that its pupils are monsters learning to coexist with humans. Here Sukune and love intrest Moka go on a series of adventures with friends while romance buds. Though there is alot of fan-service and over animation, the female leads are extreamly strong and think for themselves. This is one of my childhood favorites which I still watch now and Im giving it a ratimg of 10/10.

    Ayuzawa Misaki serves as the student council president at Seika High. However, unbeknownst to her classmates, she works part-time as an employee at a Maid Cafe.
