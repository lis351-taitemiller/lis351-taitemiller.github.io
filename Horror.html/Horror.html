Below is my current top five rankings of avalible Horror animes. The criteria for my classification is that the show includes a unpredictable plot and must be able to scare me in some way, shape, or form. From the shows I picked, there must be some creative twist. It can be focused on supernatrual, monsters, true crime, or all of the above. I know Horror as a genre is not for everyone! but please take a moment and give these a chance for a thrill! As you will see below, ill include the anime, a short summary, when it was aired, and where you can watch it.

  1. Devilman Crybaby (2018)

    2. This photo was taken from Promotional consideration Pintrest which shows A fanmade poster of the anime.

    Devilman Crybaby (2018) is a short ten episode series made by Netfix Originals. In the show, devils exist among humans and they feed off of chaos. the show starts with a weak an unassuming Akira Fudou. He wears his heart on his sleeve and is the complete opposite of his childhood friend Ryou Asuka. One day, Ryou asks Akira to attend an X rated parted called 'Sabeth' to try to catch physical evidence of devils existence. things go south at the party and Akira and Ryou end up fighting for their lives fighting grotesque monsters. This horror anime combines comedy, romance, and thrills to make a unique and beloved show. This show recived 89% Rotten Tomatos. One fan said "This anime freaked me out, made me cry, and I would watch it all over again!". overall I rate this anime a 10/10!

  2. Hellsing (2001)

    3. This photo was taken from this is from the Anime Amino and was provided by Alpha Coders.

    Hellsing (1997 to 2008) was a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Kouta Hirano. In total it is 89 episodes, all full of thrilling and horrifying vampires. This anime is set in moden- day England, where vampires and human live among eachother. Hellsing is an orginization that specializes in dealing with suoernaural threats. the CEO has vampire Alucard as her most powerful asset. Him along with a team of powerful vampires are tasked with eliminating threats to the orginization. I rate this show a 8/10. It's funny, unique, and scary with a Rotten Tomato rating of 88%.I highly recomend giving it a chance, you might find yourself doing more good deeds.

  3. Junji Ito: Japanese Tales of The Macabre (2023)

    4. This photo was taken from CrunchyRoll all original creaters- Liam Dempsy.

    Junji Itos Japanese Stories of Macabre(2023) was an adaption of Itos Swinging Ballons. This Netflix Adaption that features two of Itos short stories per episode. this is an ongoing show with over 25 episodes out currently. This show is not for the weak. Its one of the most Terrifying mangas ever wrote, Ito has the most creative use of body horror of any author ive ever read. every episode is spine tigling, and its hard writing a summary when every episode is diffrent. I rate this anime a 10/10 just because I actully had to pull the covers over my head white watching this anime. And as a horror lover, that is a hard feat!.

  4. Ergo Proxy(2006) )

    5. This photo was taken from Wallpapers.com

    Ergo Proxy (2006) is a Japanese Horror cyberpunk anime. Its produced by Manglobe and the series ran for 23 episodes. The anime is set in a post-apocalyptic future where humans and AutoReiv and robots coexist. all until thr robots gain self awarness and start murdering humans. through this, deep inverstigations are launched and new huminoid "proxy" species are discovered. This anime was ahead of its time in terms of animation. Ergo Proxy is an amazing horror anime featuring the dangereus relationship between humans and new technology. It recived a Rotten Tomato rating of 87%. This is a perfect show to binge on a cold rainy night! Im giving it a ratimg of 8.2/10.
